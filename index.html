Travel Wishlist

Your personal collection of dream destinations

About the App

Travel Wishlist is a simple browser-based app that lets you track the places you want to visit. Save destinations, write notes about why you want to go, give each place a rating, and organize everything by region — all stored directly in your browser with no account required.

Planned Features

  • Add destinations
  • Edit destinations
  • Delete destinations
  • Add notes and ratings
  • Filter by region
